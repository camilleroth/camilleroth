Job openings

Post-doctoral and doctoral positions

We are currently opening two 3-year doctoral fellowships under the ANR grant “Records” focusing on the effect of algorithms on cultural practices and specifically in the context of music streaming platforms. Please check the detailed job offers on this webpage (DL: May 31, 2020).

We also open one 3-yr post-doctoral position at Centre Marc Bloch on the economic and sociological impacts of ICT-related industries. The detailed job offer is available here (D/L: May 15, 2020).

Finally, a second wave of recruitment for doctoral and post-doctoral positions under the ERC Consolidator grant “Socsemics” may be launched around summer 2020. Please feel free to solicit me nonetheless if you think your profile might fit one of the areas of Socsemics, either at the doctoral or post-doctoral level, especially in areas related to socio-semantic network modeling, sociology of digital public spaces, computational linguistics, or hybrid network visualization.

All these offers take place in an interdisciplinary context and touch a variety of domains: computational social science, political science, NLP, information visualization, sociology of the internet, social network analysis, complex network modeling, essentially.
